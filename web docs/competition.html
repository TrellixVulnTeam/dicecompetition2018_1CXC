DICE Competition 2018

Participate in this prestigious design competition, a steppingstone to your future career.

DICE Competition 2018 is the most awaiting design competition in Malaysia, open to all engineering, computer science, IT, and science/mathematics bachelor students. Besides promoting innovative culture in design work and also searching out brightest talents for product development and further research, the most highlighted goal of the competition is to tackle real-world industry problems with smartest solutions in collaboration with technology companies, including Microsoft, Intel, Google and many more. The competition is the best platform to connect industry and technology through participants’ brilliant ideas. DICE Competition 2018 welcomes the most ambitious, creative, and smart students to participate and showcase their talents.

Why Must I Join It ?

  1. Collaboration between industry and technology through brilliant problems solvers.

  2. Supply state-of-the-art industry technology platform for students’ final year project.

  3. Provide exposure to real-world problem for students.

  4. Distinguish talents showcased by the students.

  5. Open opportunity to high-value jobs offered by participating companies.

Business Organization

Participants applies scientific knowledge and ingenuity to develop solutions for technical problems. While solutions can be developed with the platforms provided by Technology industry. But, who defines the problems?
Business organization specify their problems. The organization brings real-life problems for participants to undertake as their competition project. Companies from various industries are throwing their challenges. Applicants can choose to take up.

Once enrolled, additional support from the selected business organization may be provided. This may include technical mentorship, field trip visits, access to lab facilities and prototyping assistance. The organization are :

  • Celcom
  • Cyberview
  • Daikin
  • Telekom Malaysia
  • WCS Malaysia

Please visit Business Organization for more details

Terms & Conditions

  • Team members must be from the same university in Malaysia.
  • Each team should have 4 members.
  • There is no limit on the number of teams from each university.
  • Each team should have 2 mentors. A university mentor is the lecturer from the same university and an industry mentor is based on the technology chosen by the team.
  • Each team should choose a technology industry and tackle a problem faced by business organization.

Fee

    participation commitment.

Prizes to be win

  • Technology Industry Prizes.
    Cash prizes (total of RM10,000 / industry) are awarded to 5 finalists from each technology industry.
  • Business Organization Award.
    Cash prizes (RM3,000 each) are offered to a winner for each business organization.
